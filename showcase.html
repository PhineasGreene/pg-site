Typesetting

I offer typesetting services for things like papers, articles, or any other short to mid length literature. If you are interested in having your document typeset please email me at phineasgreene@outlook.com. My typesetting offers:

  1. Versitile styling. I can do any number of styles from modern to Victorian.
  2. Consitent, professional formatting. Your document will always have perfectly consistent formatting.
  3. Powerful typesetting. I can include graphs, tables, images, and math.
  4. Total cross platform compatability. My final product ships as a pdf file, meaning there is no variance between devices.
  5. Embeded styling. All fonts and formatting are embeded, ensuring that no matter who views the document it will maintain consistency.
  6. Perfect printing. I can typeset your document for any standard size paper (including legal) so that it will always print true.
